Design Notes

  • "When I say" / "Replace with" — inline add form at top of card, always visible
  • Aa checkbox — case-sensitive toggle. When checked, expansion only triggers on exact case match
  • Aa badge on list rows indicates case-sensitive entries
  • × button — delete with no confirmation (instant, entries are cheap to re-add)
  • Count footer — "N expansions" at bottom
  • Nav renamed: "Shortcuts" → "Expansions" with matching icon
