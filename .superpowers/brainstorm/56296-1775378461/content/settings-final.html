Design Notes

  • Greyed-out toggles (opacity 0.5) = unimplemented features — Recording indicator, Pause media, Auto-add to dictionary
  • Keybind recorder — click badge → turns purple with "Press keys...", other rows dim, hint bar appears at bottom of card
  • Esc during recording = cancel (revert to original), Backspace = clear binding
  • Settings auto-save — every change writes to JSON immediately via existing LocalSettingsStore
  • 5 cards: Keybinds, Input, App, Sound, Extras
