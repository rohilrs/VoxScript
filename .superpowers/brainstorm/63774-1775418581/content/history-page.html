Design Notes

  • Grouped by date — "Today", "Yesterday", or full date (e.g. "April 3, 2026")
  • Sorted by time — newest first within each group, time shown (e.g. "2:45 PM")
  • Full text always visible — no truncation, no expand/collapse
  • Metadata badges — model name (always), "✨ Enhanced" (if AI-enhanced)
  • Actions — copy to clipboard (📋), delete (×)
  • Search — filters across text and enhanced text
  • Pagination — "Load more" button, 20 at a time
